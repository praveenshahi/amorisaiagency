A CRM Copilot joins your sales calls, auto-updates deal records with notes and next steps, drafts your follow-up email while you're still on the call, and alerts you to deals that are going silent. Starting at ₹1.75L setup + ₹20k/month.

## What it does

The best sales reps close deals. The worst admin work is what eats their time: updating CRM after every call, writing follow-up emails, tracking which deals are stalling. This agent eliminates that overhead.

- **Auto CRM updates:** Call ends → deal stage, notes, and next steps updated in your CRM automatically
- **Follow-up drafts:** Ready within 5 minutes of call end — you review, edit, and send
- **Stall detection:** Flags deals with no activity for 7+ days, suggests next action
- **Meeting prep:** Before a call, generates a brief with: deal history, last touchpoint, open questions, context on the company
- **Pipeline health:** Weekly digest of pipeline coverage, at-risk deals, and suggested actions

## How it works

**Step 1 — Bot joins your call.** "Amoris Notes" joins Zoom or Meet as a participant. Participant sees "Recording in progress." Transcription starts.

**Step 2 — Call happens.** Agent transcribes in real time. Identifies: key discussion points, budget mentions, objections, decision timeline, agreed next steps.

**Step 3 — Post-call processing (5 minutes).** Call summary written. CRM deal record updated. Follow-up email drafted. Next meeting scheduled in calendar if agreed.

**Step 4 — Review & send.** You get a Slack/email notification: "Call summary ready. CRM updated. Draft follow-up in your Gmail drafts — review before sending."

**Step 5 — Pipeline monitoring.** Every Monday morning: pipeline health digest. Which deals moved. Which are stalling. Which need attention this week.
