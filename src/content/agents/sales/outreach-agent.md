An Outreach Agent writes a personalised first-line for every prospect based on their company context, sends sequenced emails and LinkedIn messages at the right intervals, and triages replies — so your sales team spends time on conversations, not sending. Starting at ₹2L setup + ₹25k/month.

## What it does

Spray-and-pray outreach doesn't work. Personalised outreach at scale is the only approach that does — but it's too time-consuming to do manually at volume. This agent does both.

- **Personalised first lines:** Each email opens with something specific to this prospect (recent news, funding, a LinkedIn post, a job posting signal)
- **Multi-channel sequences:** Email + LinkedIn combination, spaced for maximum reach
- **Automated follow-ups:** Sequences run automatically, paused when prospect replies
- **Reply triage:** Incoming replies read and categorised — hot leads escalated immediately
- **CRM sync:** All activity logged; replies, opens, and clicks tracked
- **A/B testing:** Tests subject lines and message variants, shifts budget to better performers

## How it works

**Step 1 — Lead list in.** From your Lead Research Agent or existing CRM. Each lead has: name, company, role, LinkedIn URL, relevant context.

**Step 2 — Personalisation generated.** For each lead, agent reads their recent LinkedIn activity, company news, and job postings — writes a specific opening line and tailored message.

**Step 3 — Sequence launched.** Emails and LinkedIn messages sent at optimal times. Sequence tracks opens, clicks, replies.

**Step 4 — Reply received.** Agent reads the reply, classifies it, updates CRM, and pings your team for hot replies.

**Step 5 — Reporting.** Weekly email: open rates, reply rates, hot leads generated, sequence performance by segment.
