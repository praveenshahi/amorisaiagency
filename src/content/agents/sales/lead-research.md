A Lead Research Agent builds a fresh list of enriched prospects matching your ICP every day — company data, decision-maker contacts, recent news signals — and pushes them directly into your CRM. Your team starts each day with warm, researched leads instead of spending hours on manual research. Starting at ₹1.5L setup + ₹20k/month.

## What it does

Manual lead research is the highest-cost, lowest-skill activity in sales. An SDR spends 3–4 hours/day researching leads they'll contact for 5 minutes. This agent does the research overnight.

- **Daily ICP-matched lists:** New leads every morning matching your defined criteria
- **Full enrichment:** Company info, decision-maker details, funding, tech stack, intent signals
- **Trigger monitoring:** Alerts when target accounts show buying signals (funding, hiring, news)
- **CRM push:** Direct integration — leads appear in your CRM ready for outreach
- **Deduplication:** Never pushes a lead already in your database
- **List hygiene:** Removes unresponsive or bounced contacts weekly

## How it works

**Step 1 — ICP definition.** You define your ideal customer: industry, company size, geography, role, tech stack used, revenue signals, recent triggers (funding, new hires, expansion).

**Step 2 — Agent runs nightly.** Pulls from LinkedIn, Apollo, Crunchbase, news sources, job boards. Filters to ICP match. Enriches every match.

**Step 3 — Morning delivery.** New leads appear in your CRM every morning. Each lead has: company overview, contact details, why they match your ICP, recent signals, and a suggested outreach angle.

**Step 4 — Trigger alerts.** If a target account raises funding or posts 5 new engineering jobs, your sales team gets a Slack alert immediately — "Company X raised $2M. They're hiring 5 engineers. This is your window."

**Step 5 — Continuous refinement.** You mark leads as good/bad fit over time. Agent learns your actual ICP from feedback and tightens targeting.
