A Lead Qualification Agent engages every visitor who raises their hand — on your website, WhatsApp, or landing page — asks your qualification questions conversationally, scores them against your ICP, and either books a meeting for hot leads or adds cold ones to nurture. Starting at ₹1.5L setup + ₹20k/month.

## What it does

Inbound leads go cold fast. Every hour of delay costs conversion. Most teams can't respond to every lead in minutes — and when they do, half the leads aren't qualified. This agent handles qualification at volume, instantly.

- **Instant engagement:** Responds to every lead within 5 seconds of form submit or website chat
- **Conversational qualification:** Asks your 4–6 qualification questions naturally, not as a survey
- **Lead scoring:** Scores leads against your ICP definition. Hot / Warm / Cold labels applied
- **Hot lead routing:** Immediately books a demo or alerts your sales team via Slack/CRM
- **Nurture entry:** Cold/warm leads entered into your email or WhatsApp nurture sequence
- **24/7 operation:** Qualifies a Sunday evening lead the same as a Monday morning one

## How it works

**Step 1 — Lead arrives.** Fills a form, starts a website chat, or messages your WhatsApp number.

**Step 2 — Agent engages.** "Hi, thanks for reaching out to Amoris. Quick question — what's the main thing you're trying to solve?"

**Step 3 — Qualification conversation.** Over 3–5 exchanges, agent covers: pain point, company size/context, timeline, budget signal, decision authority.

**Step 4 — Scoring.** Answers mapped to your ICP criteria. Score calculated. Label applied.

**Step 5 — Routing.** Hot lead (score 70+) → demo booked in calendar, Slack alert to sales. Warm (40–69) → follow-up task created in CRM. Cold (<40) → nurture sequence.
