A Website Support Agent is trained on your specific documentation, FAQs, and policies. It gives accurate answers (not hallucinations), qualifies leads inline, and transfers to humans only when genuinely needed. Deflects 60–80% of tickets. Starting at ₹1L setup + ₹15k/month.

## What it does

Generic chatbots fail because they guess. This agent only answers from what you've approved — your product docs, FAQs, pricing pages, policy documents. Accuracy rate is 90%+ for trained questions.

- **Accurate answers:** Grounded in your knowledge base, with source citations on request
- **Lead qualification:** Asks the right questions and pushes qualified leads to your CRM
- **Ticket deflection:** Resolves common questions without creating a support ticket
- **Seamless handoff:** Transfers to Freshdesk, Intercom, or your team inbox with full conversation history
- **Available 24/7:** Handles overnight and weekend queries without on-call support staff

## How it works

**Step 1 — Knowledge base setup.** You share your docs (PDFs, Notion, Google Docs, website pages). We ingest, chunk, and index them. Setup takes 1–2 weeks.

**Step 2 — Agent deployed on your website.** A small script added to your site. Works with your existing design — customisable widget.

**Step 3 — Customer asks a question.** Agent searches the knowledge base, finds the relevant answer, and responds in 1–2 seconds.

**Step 4 — Complex queries.** Agent recognises when a question is outside its training. Instead of guessing, it says "let me connect you with the team" and creates a ticket.

**Step 5 — Continuous improvement.** Every unanswered question is flagged for you to add to the knowledge base. The agent gets better over time.
