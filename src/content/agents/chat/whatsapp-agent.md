A WhatsApp Business Agent handles your customer conversations end-to-end inside WhatsApp: catalog browsing, order taking, payment collection, order tracking, and support — without a human agent for 80% of queries. Starting at ₹1.25L setup + ₹15k/month.

## What it does

Your customers are on WhatsApp. 500M+ Indians use it daily. But most businesses leave WhatsApp as a one-way broadcast channel or route everything to a human agent. This agent turns it into a full commerce and support channel.

- **Product catalog:** Customer asks about products, agent shares images, descriptions, and prices from your catalog
- **Order taking:** Customer selects items, agent builds the order, confirms address, and sends payment link
- **Payment collection:** Razorpay/PayU link inside the chat — no redirects
- **Order status:** Customer asks "where's my order?" — agent pulls real-time status from Shiprocket
- **Support:** Returns, exchanges, complaints handled with defined resolution flows
- **Follow-ups:** Post-delivery feedback, reorder nudges, cart abandonment recovery

## How it works

**Step 1 — Customer messages your WhatsApp number.** Could be a new inquiry, support question, or order follow-up.

**Step 2 — Agent responds instantly.** Identifies intent (buy / track / support / general) and routes the conversation accordingly.

**Step 3 — Task completion.** Order built. Payment link sent. Tracking number shared. Complaint logged.

**Step 4 — Human handoff when needed.** Angry customer, complex issue, or explicit request → transferred to human with full context.

**Step 5 — CRM sync.** Every interaction logged. New customers added. Orders tracked. You see everything in your CRM dashboard.

## Languages & integrations

**Languages:** Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada (auto-detected per message)

**Commerce:** Razorpay, PayU, PayTM (payment links), Shiprocket (order tracking)

**CRM:** Zoho CRM, HubSpot, or Google Sheets for order logging

**Catalog:** Connected to your existing product database or maintained in a structured sheet
