An Instagram DM Agent responds to every message and relevant comment on your Instagram account — handles product questions, collects contact details, moves high-intent followers to WhatsApp, and logs leads in your CRM. Starting at ₹1L setup + ₹15k/month.

## What it does

Most business Instagram accounts have a leaky funnel: followers ask questions in DMs that go unanswered for hours. Story replies get ignored. High-intent comments get a 👍 and nothing else. Every missed message is a potential customer who moved on.

- **Instant DM responses:** Responds to every new DM within 30 seconds
- **Product inquiries:** Answers price, availability, and shipping questions from your catalog
- **Lead capture:** Collects name, contact number, and requirement from interested followers
- **WhatsApp migration:** Moves high-intent conversations to WhatsApp for payment/closure
- **Story reply handling:** Responds to story replies with relevant information or links
- **Comment engagement:** Replies to qualifying comments (pricing questions, availability queries)

## How it works

**Step 1 — Follower messages.** "Hey, how much is the blue dress?" or "Do you ship to Pune?"

**Step 2 — Agent responds instantly.** Pulls from your product catalog or FAQ. Personalised per message, not templated-looking.

**Step 3 — Qualification.** If the person shows purchase intent, agent asks 2 questions: what are you looking for, and what's your contact number for order confirmation?

**Step 4 — CRM entry.** Lead logged with Instagram handle, contact number, and what they were interested in.

**Step 5 — WhatsApp transfer.** For order-ready conversations, agent shares your WhatsApp number (or WhatsApp chat link) where the purchase can be completed.
