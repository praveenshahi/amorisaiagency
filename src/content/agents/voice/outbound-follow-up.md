An Outbound Follow-Up Agent calls every new lead within 5 minutes of form submission, qualifies them with 3–4 questions, and books meetings only for leads who are ready to buy. Your sales team stops wasting time on cold discovery and starts every call warmed up. Starting at ₹2L setup + ₹30k/month.

## The problem it solves

Speed-to-lead determines conversion. 78% of B2B deals go to the vendor who responds first. Most sales teams respond in 4–8 hours. This agent responds in under 5 minutes — then qualifies while the lead is still at their desk thinking about you.

## How it works

**Step 1 — Lead submits a form.** On your website, Facebook Lead Ads, IndiaMart, JustDial — any source with a webhook.

**Step 2 — Agent calls within 5 minutes.** "Hi, this is Aria from [Company]. You just enquired about [product/service] — got 2 minutes to answer a few quick questions?"

**Step 3 — Qualification.** 3–4 questions tuned to your ICP: budget range, timeline, current situation, decision authority. Answers logged in CRM in real time.

**Step 4 — Outcome.** Hot lead → booked directly into sales team's calendar. Not ready → added to nurture sequence. Wrong fit → marked disqualified. No pickup → 3 retries + WhatsApp.

**Step 5 — Sales team gets context.** Before their booked call, sales rep gets: lead's answers, company info, call recording summary. No cold starts.

## What it replaces

A BDE doing outbound follow-up handles ~30–40 calls/day with 30–40% connect rate. This agent handles 300+ leads/day with consistent messaging. Your BDE time moves to closing, not chasing.
