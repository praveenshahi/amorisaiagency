A Payment & Reminder Agent makes pre-due, same-day, and early overdue calls to your customers at scale — in their language, at the right time, every time. Collections and renewal rates improve without adding collections staff. Starting at ₹2L setup + ₹30k/month.

## What it does

Collections leakage is usually not a willingness problem — it's a reminder problem. Customers forget. They get busy. They meant to pay. A timely, friendly call converts 40–60% of pre-due reminders.

This agent handles:
- **EMI/payment reminders:** Calls 3 days before, on the day, and 1–3 days after if unpaid
- **Renewal nudges:** Calls 14, 7, and 3 days before subscription/policy expiry
- **Post-payment confirmation:** "Did you receive our WhatsApp payment confirmation? Do you need the receipt?"
- **Feedback calls:** "Your loan was disbursed last week — how was your experience?" (NPS data collected)

## How it works

**Step 1 — Agent reads your list.** You share a CRM export or Google Sheet with: customer name, due date, amount, phone number, language preference.

**Step 2 — Scheduled calls.** Agent calls at the times you configure. No manual dialling. 200 calls can be queued and executed in 2 hours.

**Step 3 — Conversation.** "Hi Ramesh ji, this is Priya calling from [Company]. Your EMI of ₹4,200 is due on Thursday. I'm sending the payment link to your WhatsApp now." 

**Step 4 — Outcomes logged.** Paid → logged. Promise to pay → flagged with date. Dispute → escalated to human. No answer → retry at alternate time.

**Step 5 — Payment link delivery.** WhatsApp payment link sent immediately after call, with reference number and due amount.
