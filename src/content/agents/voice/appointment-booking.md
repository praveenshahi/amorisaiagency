An AI Appointment Booking Agent handles every booking call end-to-end: checks your calendar for availability, confirms the slot, and sends the caller a WhatsApp confirmation — all while your staff focuses on the appointment in front of them. Starting at ₹1.5L setup + ₹25k/month.

## What it does

Manual booking is a coordination tax. Phones ring during appointments. Staff misquote availability. Patients book, forget, and don't show. This agent eliminates every friction point.

- Shows real-time availability from your calendar
- Books the slot and immediately blocks it
- Sends a WhatsApp confirmation to the caller
- Sends reminder calls 24 hours and 2 hours before the appointment
- Handles rescheduling and cancellations without staff involvement
- Manages waitlists automatically when slots are full

## How it works

**Step 1 — Caller says why they're calling.** "I need to book an appointment with Dr. Sharma" or "Can I schedule a facial for Saturday?"

**Step 2 — Agent checks live availability.** Pulls from your calendar in real time. Offers 2–3 options. ("I have slots at 10am, 2pm, and 4pm on Saturday. Which works?")

**Step 3 — Caller confirms.** Agent books the slot, blocks it immediately, and confirms with the caller's name and number.

**Step 4 — Confirmations sent.** WhatsApp confirmation to the caller. Calendar event updated. Staff sees the booking on their end.

**Step 5 — Reminders.** Automated reminder call 24 hours before. SMS/WhatsApp 2 hours before. No-show rate drops 30–50%.

## What it replaces

A dedicated booking coordinator costs ₹18–22k/month and handles roughly 80–100 bookings/day. This agent handles the same volume 24/7 at ₹25k/month — with no sick days, no training time, and no "she's on lunch" dead zones.
