An AI Receptionist answers every inbound call to your business, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. It greets callers in their language, routes them correctly, books appointments directly into your calendar, and sends you a summary of every call. Starting at ₹1.5L setup + ₹20k/month.

## What it does

A missed call is a missed patient, missed lead, missed booking. Most service businesses miss 30–40% of inbound calls because phones ring during appointments, after hours, or when the front desk is occupied.

This agent:
- Answers every call within 2 rings
- Introduces itself naturally ("Hi, you've reached [Clinic Name], how can I help you?")
- Qualifies why the caller is calling
- Books, reschedules, or cancels appointments directly into your calendar system
- Routes urgent calls to your mobile immediately
- Takes a message and sends it via WhatsApp when no action is needed

## How it works

**Step 1 — Call arrives.** Caller dials your existing number (no number change needed). The agent picks up in under 2 seconds.

**Step 2 — Intent detection.** In the first exchange, the agent identifies: appointment booking, existing appointment query, directions/hours, complaint, or other. 

**Step 3 — Resolution.** Booking requests go to your calendar in real time. Queries are answered from your knowledge base. Complaints are escalated to a human immediately.

**Step 4 — Post-call.** Every call gets a 3-line WhatsApp summary to your team: who called, what they wanted, what was done.
