An Invoice & Data Entry Agent reads your invoices, receipts, and forms — extracts vendor name, amounts, dates, GST details, and all structured fields — and enters them into Zoho Books, Tally, or your accounting system automatically. Starting at ₹1.5L setup + ₹20k/month.

## What it does

Data entry is the work that has to happen but adds no value. An accounts executive spending 3 hours/day entering invoices into Tally isn't doing accounts work — they're doing transcription. This agent handles the transcription.

- **Invoice processing:** Reads PDF and scanned invoices, extracts all fields, enters into your accounting system
- **PO matching:** Cross-references invoices against purchase orders, flags mismatches
- **GST compliance:** Captures GSTIN, HSN codes, tax breakdown correctly for Indian invoices
- **Batch processing:** Process a folder of 100 invoices overnight — ready by morning
- **Exception handling:** Low-confidence extractions flagged for human review, not silently entered
- **Duplicate detection:** Flags invoices that look like duplicates before entry

## How it works

**Step 1 — Invoice arrives.** Via email attachment, shared folder, WhatsApp image, or direct upload.

**Step 2 — Processing.** Agent reads the document. Extracts: vendor name, GSTIN, invoice number, date, line items, amounts, GST, total.

**Step 3 — Validation.** Cross-checks against your vendor master. Flags unknown vendors. Checks for PO match if required.

**Step 4 — System entry.** Approved records entered into Zoho Books, Tally, QuickBooks, or your system. Rejected records go to a review queue.

**Step 5 — Confirmation.** Daily summary email: X invoices processed, Y entered, Z flagged for review.
