A Reporting Agent connects to your CRM, ad platforms, analytics tools, and spreadsheets — generates daily and weekly business reports with written commentary — and delivers them to you and your team on schedule. No one opens a spreadsheet. Starting at ₹1L setup + ₹15k/month.

## What it does

The right data, at the right time, in readable form is worth more than any dashboard. This agent assembles your numbers from where they live, adds context, and sends the report — so everyone starts the day with the same picture.

- **Daily briefings:** What happened yesterday. Pipeline, leads, ad spend, revenue — in one email
- **Weekly reviews:** Performance vs targets, top wins, areas behind plan
- **Client reports:** Branded weekly/monthly reports for your clients — pulled from live data
- **Automated board prep:** Monthly metrics prepared in a format ready for investor/board review
- **Anomaly alerts:** If a metric drops sharply (leads down 40%, ROAS drops below threshold), you get an immediate alert — not just the weekly report

## How it works

**Step 1 — Connect data sources.** Google Sheets, HubSpot, Meta Ads Manager, Google Analytics, Shopify — any source with an API.

**Step 2 — Define report templates.** You tell us what metrics matter, in what format, with what commentary. We build the templates during setup.

**Step 3 — Agent runs on schedule.** Daily at 8am or weekly on Monday morning (your choice). Pulls live data from all sources.

**Step 4 — Report generated with commentary.** Numbers plus 1-sentence interpretations. Anomalies flagged. Trends called out.

**Step 5 — Delivered.** Email to your inbox, PDF attachment, Slack message to your team channel, or Google Slide deck updated and shared.
