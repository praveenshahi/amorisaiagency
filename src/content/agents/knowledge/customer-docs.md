A Customer Docs Agent sits on your help centre and answers customer questions from your documentation. Customers stop waiting for a human to answer "how do I reset my password" — and your support team focuses on the questions that actually need judgment. Starting at ₹1.75L setup + ₹20k/month.

## What it does

Support tickets cost ₹150–500 each to resolve when you factor in agent time. 60–70% of tickets are repetitive questions answered in your help docs — if customers could find them. This agent makes your help docs immediately accessible through conversation.

- **Natural language Q&A:** "How do I export my data as CSV?" not "find article about export"
- **Contextual follow-ups:** Customer can ask follow-up questions in the same conversation
- **Multi-article synthesis:** Pulls from multiple help articles to answer complex questions
- **Ticket creation:** Creates a support ticket when the agent can't resolve the issue
- **Feedback loop:** Poor answers flagged for documentation improvement
- **Available 24/7:** No support hours. No timezone limitations.

## How it works

**Step 1 — Help documentation ingested.** Connect your Freshdesk/Zendesk knowledge base, Notion help centre, Confluence docs, or website help pages.

**Step 2 — Deployed on your help centre.** Embedded as a chat widget or full-page interface. Styled to match your brand.

**Step 3 — Customer asks a question.** "I was charged twice this month" or "How do I add team members?"

**Step 4 — Agent answers from docs.** Retrieves relevant documentation, synthesises a clear answer, links to the full article for reference.

**Step 5 — Escalation path.** Can't resolve → agent creates a ticket with conversation history, priority set by sentiment analysis (frustrated customer = high priority).
