An Internal Knowledge Agent is your company's searchable brain. Staff ask questions in plain language — "what's the process for client onboarding?", "how many leaves do we get for marriage?" — and get accurate answers from your actual documents, with the source cited. Starting at ₹2L setup + ₹25k/month.

## What it does

Every company has knowledge locked in documents that nobody reads. New employees ask the same questions 50 times. Experienced employees spend 20 minutes hunting for a policy. Managers repeat themselves on every call.

This agent makes your existing documentation actually useful:

- **Instant policy lookups:** HR policies, leave rules, expense guidelines answered in seconds
- **Process guidance:** "How do I onboard a new client?" — answered with the exact SOP steps
- **Product knowledge:** Sales team asks product questions and gets accurate specs, not guesses
- **Onboarding acceleration:** New joinees can self-serve 80% of first-week questions
- **Source citations:** Every answer links back to the source document section

## How it works

**Step 1 — Knowledge base ingestion.** You share your documents. We ingest, chunk, and index them into a searchable vector store. 1–2 weeks for initial setup.

**Step 2 — Staff access via Slack/Teams or web.** Deployed as a Slack bot, Teams bot, or web app accessible on your internal tools page.

**Step 3 — Question asked.** "What are the grounds for immediate termination?" Staff member types in plain language.

**Step 4 — Agent retrieves and answers.** Searches knowledge base, finds relevant sections, synthesises a clear answer. Cites: "Source: HR Handbook v3.2, Section 6.4."

**Step 5 — Knowledge gaps flagged.** Questions the agent can't answer are logged weekly for your HR/ops team to add to the knowledge base.

## Languages & integrations

**Languages:** Hindi, English

**Document sources:** Notion, Confluence, Google Drive, SharePoint, Dropbox, direct uploads

**Access:** Slack bot, Microsoft Teams bot, or web interface

**Role-based access:** Documents tagged by department; staff only access their permitted content
